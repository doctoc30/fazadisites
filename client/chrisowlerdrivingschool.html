About Chris Fowler

Your Automatic Specialist

Chris Fowler is a fully qualified ADI (Approved Driving Instructor) specialising exclusively in automatic driving lessons. Based in Great Blakenham, Ipswich, Chris covers Ipswich, Stowmarket and surrounding areas of Suffolk.

After training locally through the Benson School of Motoring, Chris established his independent practice. He’s highly recommended for beginners and nervous or unconfident drivers, maintaining a strong first-time pass rate at Ipswich test centre.
