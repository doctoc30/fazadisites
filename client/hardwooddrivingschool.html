Frequently Asked Questions

Are your instructors DVSA-Approved?

Yes, all our instructors are fully qualified and registered with the Driving Instructors Association (DIA).

What areas do you cover specifically?

We cover Bolton, Bury, Manchester, Rochdale, Heywood, Bamford, and Radcliffe.

Do you offer intensive courses?

Yes, we offer fast-track intensive driving courses designed for those looking to pass quickly.

Can I get help with my theory test?

Absolutely! All students get free access to Theory Test Pro to help them prepare.

What is your "client-first" approach?

It means we tailor every lesson to your individual learning style and pace, ensuring maximum progress.
